How We Price Partnerships

What does it cost? +

Our ongoing partnerships typically start at $3,500/month, with many brands investing more for dedicated support, strategy, and execution. We’ll recommend the right plan based on your needs — whether it’s consistent monthly momentum or scaling for growth..

What impacts the monthly rate?+

Your monthly rate is based on the size and complexity of your digital presence, the amount of design, development, or marketing work required, and how quickly you want to move. We’ll align the plan to match your objectives and internal resources.

Do you work with large retainers?+

Yes. Many of our clients engage us at $10,000 per month and up, where we function as an embedded digital and marketing partner. This allows us to create the most impact, handle larger initiatives, and maintain flexibility as priorities shift.

What if I’m not ready for a monthly retainer?+

We also offer project-based pricing for companies starting with a specific initiative, like a website redesign or marketing campaign. This is a great way to experience our team before scaling into a long-term partnership.
